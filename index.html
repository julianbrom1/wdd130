Colombia

Colombia is a diverse and vibrant country in the northwest of South America, bordered by the Caribbean Sea to the north and the Pacific Ocean to the west. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Colombia boasts a mix of indigenous, Spanish, and African influences. Its landscape is incredibly varied, with the Andes mountains, Amazon rainforest, plains (Llanos), and beautiful beaches.
